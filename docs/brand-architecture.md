# Brand Architecture

## Naming Rule

Use this naming split consistently:

- `Black Box Labs`: lab umbrella, repository, and narrative frame
- `INK Receipts`: shared proof primitive
- `BLKBXS SDK`: banking-facing developer product
- `MAND8 SDK`: insurance-facing developer product
- `DUE SDK`: legal-facing developer product

Do not collapse those into a single master product. In particular:

- DUE is not a subsection of MAND8.
- MAND8 is not the umbrella name for every market.
- BLKBXS is not the umbrella name for all products.

## Market Language

Each product should speak in the language of the institution that needs to rely on the evidence.

### BLKBXS

- banking controls
- oversight
- exception handling
- operational evidence

### MAND8

- insurability
- exposure unit
- underwriting evidence
- claims defensibility
- renewal evidence
- exclusions
- policy conditions
- risk controls
- actuarial signal export
- portfolio monitoring

MAND8 is UK-first by design and should explicitly speak to London market delegated authority workflows where relevant.

### DUE

- defensibility
- matter context
- authority
- privilege
- disclosure
- discovery
- litigation hold
- chain of custody
- human review
- legal reliance
- materiality
- adverse action
- audit trail
- dispute readiness
- liability review

## Positioning Boundary

Avoid leading product copy with generic umbrella terms such as:

- AI governance
- explainability
- compliance platform
- trust layer

Lead with the institutional question each slice answers instead.
